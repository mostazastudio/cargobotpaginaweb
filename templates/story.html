OUR ROAD MAP

TAKE THE RIDE WITH US

In 2014, the co-founders of CargoBot with more than 20 years of experience in technology, freight brokering, technology development, and consulting services world met as real time users and intermediaries between shippers and carriers.

By 2015, they began the first steps in the creation of CargoBot. Finding the need to improve the process of the FTL inland freight services in the Unites States, they began with the creation of an idea that would integrate all of the process in one for both shippers and carriers by relying on technology.

In 2016, the portals for shippers and fleet companies is created as well as the app for fleet drivers and owner operators. CargoBot is launched to the market in May of 2017.

3TN

TRANSPARENT TRUCK TRANSPORTATION NETWORK

Transparent truck transportation network (abbreviated 3TN) in logistics and supply chain management is a company’s outsource of trucks that work under a network which operates all of its business in a transparent manner, in order to support a company’s distribution and fulfillment services.

3TN’s specialize in providing access to its customers to a network of trucks for their transportation needs in a transparent and technology based operation. 3TN’s run under a transparent business focus; providing its customers with a platform that connects them to thousands of carriers nationwide, allows them to post their loads for bidding, allows them to keep and see an electronic version of all of the business documents (rate of confirmation, BOL, POD), provides them access to the carriers information from US DOT/MC number to all of the carriers insurance, exact location of their truck once business has been established, as well as direct communication. Often, these services go beyond logistics and include value added services such as a back office tracking all of the loads, extra cargo insurance, direct customer service access on each platform.
